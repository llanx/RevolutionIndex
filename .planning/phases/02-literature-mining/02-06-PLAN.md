**Variable-Framework Map:**

Create a matrix table showing:
| Variable Category | Variable | PSI | PLI | FSP | PITF | V-Dem | FSI | Funke | Chenoweth | [other frameworks] |
with checkmarks/crosses showing which frameworks use each variable. This visually reveals: (a) which variables are used by multiple frameworks (high consensus), (b) which frameworks rely on unique variables (potential value-add), (c) which variables are "orphans" not used by any framework.

**Gap Identification:**

Identify three types of gaps:
1. **Theory gaps**: Theory suggests a variable matters, but no established measurement exists (e.g., "revolutionary consciousness," "elite legitimacy")
2. **Data gaps**: Measurement exists but no known data source for the US (e.g., some V-Dem sub-indicators, campaign-level mobilization data)
3. **Coverage gaps**: The 6 domain reviews may have missed important literature -- identify areas where coverage is thinnest and flag for future investigation

**Phase 1 Open Questions -- provide explicit responses:**

1. Updated Turchin PSI operationalizations since End Times (2023) -- synthesize findings from Domain 1 review
2. Other prospect theory applications to political risk -- synthesize findings from Domain 4 review
3. Financial stress -> political mobilization evidence -- synthesize findings from Domains 3 and 4
4. Alternative data sources for discontinued series -- synthesize from MCP cross-referencing results in catalog
5. Normalization methods for trending series -- synthesize from Domain 4 and framework assessment (COINr, FSI methodology)
6. Frameworks beyond the existing 3 -- directly reference framework assessment document
7. Sensitivity analysis methods for composite indicators -- synthesize from framework assessment and Domain 4

**Recommendations must be actionable:**
- Phase 3 recommendations should say "prioritize these N variables for data sourcing because they have Strong evidence and fed-data tags" -- not just "source data for variables"
- Phase 4 recommendations should say "the evidence supports considering [specific frameworks] alongside the existing 3 because [specific reasons]" -- not just "consider alternatives"
- Phase 5 recommendations should say "use V-Dem and ACLED for [specific validation purposes], but acknowledge [specific limitations]" -- not just "validate with data"

**Anti-patterns to avoid:**
- Do NOT just concatenate summaries from other documents. The synthesis adds value by CONNECTING findings across documents and drawing conclusions that no single document could.
- Do NOT introduce new variables or frameworks not already in the catalog/assessment. The synthesis maps what exists.
- Do NOT make model selection decisions. That is Phase 4's job. The synthesis presents the evidence; downstream phases decide what to do with it.
